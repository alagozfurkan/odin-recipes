Moussaka

Description

Greek Moussaka (mousaka) is without a doubt, Greece’s most popular, traditional dish! You’ll be hard pressed to find a taverna that doesn’t serve it or a household that doesn’t make it on special occasions!

Ingredients

  • eggplants
  • potatoes
  • beef
  • lamb
  • mince sauce
  • bechamel sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C/gas mark 4.
  2. Peel the potatoes.
  3. Season and roast in the oven for 35–40 minutes, until golden.
  4. Meanwhile, peel and finely chop the onions and garlic.
  5. Break everything up with a wooden spoon and turn the heat up a little.
  6. Stir in the purée, then add the wine and bring to the boil.
  7. Remove from the oven and leave to cool for at least 15 minutes before serving, if not longer.
